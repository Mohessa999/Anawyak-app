Privacy Policy

Ana Wyak protects your private couple space with local-first storage and transparent support.

Local Data Storage

All personal data and relationship content created in Ana Wyak stays on your device unless you choose to export it. We do not share or sell your memories, chats, habits, or secret language entries.

AI Service

AI responses are handled through a secure proxy or API key system. No relationship content is stored outside your device unless you explicitly use an external service or export your data.

Payments

Premium payment processing will be handled securely through Paddle checkout when subscriptions launch. We do not store credit card information on our servers.

Contact & Support
